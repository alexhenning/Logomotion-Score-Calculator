Usage:
  • Left click on a peg to cycle through the three normal tubes: triangle, circle and square.
  • Right click on a peg to place or remove an ubertube.
  • Click the race buttons to toggle whether or not you won a race.
  • Your score should appear in the lower right hand corner.

Note: According to the rules you can't place more than three ubertubes or win more to spots in the minibot race, however, there is currently nothing preventing you from doing either of those in this calculator.

If none of this makes sense to you, you should check FIRST robotics.

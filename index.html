Usage:
  • Left click on a peg to cycle through the three normal tubes: triangle, circle and square.
  • Drag the yellow ubertubes from the bin onto the pegs to place them. You can drag them off to remove them.
  • Click the race buttons to toggle whether or not you won a race.
  • Your score should appear in the lower right hand corner.
